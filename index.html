About

To be honest, you know that hardly anyone is going to read hundreds of pages of your PhD thesis. A thesis topic is specific. Reading takes too long. An intriguing content is likely to be only in Acknowledgements section. Images, video clips, animations and other media material might be an entertaining piece of story. A web page is just a right place to exhibit all such media output.

This web site is about PhD thesis by Andrey Ziyatdinov Biomimetic set up for chemosensor-based machine olfaction. Here both formal material and media content related to the thesis are presented.

Publications

Main results of the thesis are reported in three journal articles and one book chapter. The book chapter contains a description of hardware and software of the assembled set up. The journal articles cover the results related to the data analysis with emphasis on creating the data simulation tool. The three journal articles were published in the course of the thesis and enabled to present the thesis as a collection of published articles, according to the UPC statement.
